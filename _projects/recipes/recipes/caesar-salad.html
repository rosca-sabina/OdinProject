Caesar Salad

Description

Chicken Caesar Salad is an easy favorite dinner or lunch. Juicy seasoned chicken breasts served over a crisp salad with a quick caesar dressing.

This is the perfect meal sized salad and full of flavor. Serve with garlic toast.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Caesar Dressing
    • Mayonnaise
    • A clove of garlic
    • Lemon juice
    • Dijon mustard
    • Anchovies
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Season and pan-fry chicken in a skillet per recipe below then bake in the oven.
  2. Whisk dressing ingredients in a small bowl.
  3. Cut chicken and romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Toss ingredients and serve!
